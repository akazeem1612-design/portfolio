# Zeem AI Home Services Lead Ops System

## Project Type
AI automation / CRM workflow / lead operations prototype

## Status
Simulated prototype based on a home services AI automation offer.

## Problem
Home service businesses lose revenue when calls are missed, website inquiries are not followed up quickly, or leads are not routed into a clear booking workflow.

## Goal
Design a lead operations system that captures leads, qualifies urgency, updates CRM stages, triggers follow-ups, and supports appointment booking for HVAC and plumbing businesses.

## Tools Used
Make.com workflow design, CRM logic, Google Sheets sample pipeline, AI qualification concept, dashboarding.

## Simulated Results
- 5 sample home services leads.
- 4 pipeline stages.
- 3 follow-up actions.
- Priority queue for high-value leads.
- Workflow from inquiry to booking.

## Limitations
The prototype is simulated and not connected to live Twilio, GoHighLevel, or appointment-booking APIs.
