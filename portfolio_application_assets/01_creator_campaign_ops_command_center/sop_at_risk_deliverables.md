# SOP: At-Risk Deliverable Monitoring

## Purpose
Prevent creator campaign delays by identifying deliverables that are close to their due date or overdue.

## Daily Check
1. Open Creator Campaign Ops Command Center.
2. Review the At Risk Deliverables view.
3. Check each deliverable's creator, campaign, due date, status, and approval status.
4. Trigger follow-up if the deliverable is due within 48 hours and not completed.

## Automation
Make.com searches the Airtable At Risk Deliverables view and creates a Gmail draft alert with creator, campaign, due date, status, and recommended next action.
