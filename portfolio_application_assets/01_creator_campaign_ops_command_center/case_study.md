# Creator Campaign Ops Command Center

## Project Type
Operations automation / creator campaign management / workflow systems

## Status
Prototype built with simulated campaign data.

## Problem
Creator-led UGC campaigns involve many moving parts: brand requests, creator onboarding, content drafts, approvals, posting timelines, performance reporting, and payout accuracy. Without a structured backend, delays and missed handoffs can quickly create operational chaos.

## Goal
Build a campaign operations backend that helps an operations associate track creator deliverables, flag bottlenecks, monitor campaign health, and automate follow-up alerts.

## Tools Used
Airtable, Make.com, Gmail, formulas, dashboard/interface design, workflow documentation.

## What I Built
- Airtable base with Brands, Campaigns, Creators, Deliverables, and Payouts tables.
- Status formulas for At Risk and Overdue deliverables.
- Airtable Interface dashboard called Creator Campaign Ops Command Center.
- Payout logic that calculates bonus and total payout based on conversions.
- Campaign health logic based on total conversions.
- Make.com automation that searches At Risk Deliverables and creates a Gmail draft alert.

## Simulated Results
- 3 brands tracked.
- 3 campaigns managed.
- 6 creators assigned.
- 6 deliverables monitored.
- 6 payout records calculated.
- 1 Make.com alert automation built.

## Why It Matters
This demonstrates campaign tracking, creator coordination, delivery timeline management, automation, KPI reporting, payout logic, and SOP-driven execution.

## Limitations
The dataset is simulated and the system is a prototype. It does not yet integrate with a real creator submission form, ad platform metrics, or payment processor.
