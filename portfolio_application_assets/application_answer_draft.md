# Application Answer Draft: Why are you interested in this role?

I am interested in this role because it sits exactly at the intersection of operations, automation, creator-led campaigns, and systems building. What stood out to me is that the role is not just about coordinating tasks; it is about taking ownership of the backend systems that keep campaigns moving from onboarding to payout.

To prepare for roles like this, I built a simulated Creator Campaign Ops Command Center. It tracks brands, campaigns, creators, deliverables, approval status, campaign health, and payout logic inside Airtable. I also built a Make.com automation that searches for at-risk deliverables and creates a Gmail draft alert for follow-up. The project helped me think through the same problems this role would face: how to prevent delays, keep CRM data clean, track creator output, monitor campaign performance, and turn messy workflows into scalable SOPs.

I would bring strong systems thinking, comfort with tools like Airtable, Make.com, Google Sheets, and automation workflows, and the ability to translate operational chaos into clear processes. I am especially interested in MediaMaxxing because creator-led performance campaigns require both speed and structure, and I enjoy building the systems that allow teams to scale without losing control of execution.
